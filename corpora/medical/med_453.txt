kannur medical college is a medical college located in anjarakandy
spread across a 218 acre campus, this is the largest self financed medical college in north kerala. 
the hospital attached to the medical college comprises the following departments. 

general medicine
general surgery
orthopaedics
paediatrics
obstetrics and gynaecology
otorhinolaryngology (ent) & head and neck surgery
ophthalmology
dermatology
psychiatry
neurosurgery
neurology
nephrology
urology
cardiologythe hospital has 1000 beds with spacious wards and special rooms.
