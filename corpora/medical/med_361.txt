dell seton medical center at the university of texas is a hospital on the property of the dell medical school in austin, texas. it is the teaching hospital for the medical school.it is on land owned by the university of texas at austin. central health leases the land, and in turn the owner and operator of the hospital building, seton healthcare family, subleases it from central health.dell seton is a level 1 trauma center serving 11 counties in central texas. it is a comprehensive stroke center and stemi center. in may of 2023 dell seton opened the first neuro - icu in central texas.

history
the michael & susan dell foundation donated $25 million to the hospital in 2016.it opened on may 21, 2017, replacing brackenridge hospital.
