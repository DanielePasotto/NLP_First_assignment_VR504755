the melkite greek catholic patriarchal dependent territory of jerusalem (latin: hierosolymitana melchitarum) is a branch of the melkite greek catholic church immediately subject to the patriarch of antioch of the melkites. in 2023, yasser ayyash is the vicar apostolic of the patriarch youssef absi.its headquarters is the cathedral of our lady of the annunciation, jerusalem.
