the liberal catholic church international (lcci) is an independent catholic denomination within the liberal catholic movement, headquartered in casa grande, arizona, usa. following a dispute within the liberal catholic church established by bishop j.i. wedgewood, the liberal catholic church international was founded. in contrast with roman catholicism, this church and tradition endorses individual liberty and conscience, similar to congregationalist protestants.
